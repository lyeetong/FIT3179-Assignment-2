Global Mortality By Diet and Physical Practices Habits

Mortality has always been a sober issue of concern globally. Not only does it reflect the social condition and social development of a country, it is more vital in reflecting a country's citizens' health status. Mortality is usually picturized as connected with health problems and accidents. However, consequences brought by poor diet and unhealthy physical habits shall not be neglected and they are our focus in this visualisation.
